A mini social media ad-content for a local brand from Makassar called Guesswhat. It showcases a new single tee release of the Mask.v1 tee from Guesswhat and explained the context behind the design Mask.v1 and what it represents.

Guesswhat Presents: The Day the Earth Stood Still [GW_PHASE2] (2022)


Jason’s very first time playing in a theater play presented by Teater KataK. Shakal tells a love story hindered by caste differences in ancient India. In his first ever theater play, Jason Kirana got his role as a wild bandit named Schar from the Tiragaya clan. He and his team are always on a mission to raid every villages scattered across India.
